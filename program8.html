Wayanad is a district in the north-east of the Indian state of Kerala, with administrative headquarters at the municipality of Kalpetta.
The Wayanad Plateau forms a continuation of the Mysore Plateau, the southern portion of Deccan Plateau which links the Western Ghats with the Eastern Ghats.
There are many indigenous tribes in this area.The Kabini River, a tributary of Kaveri River, originates at Wayanad.
